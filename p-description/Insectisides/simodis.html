Product Description

About Product

  • Simodis Insecticide is a robust & 360°solution meticulously designed to protect your crops against a variety of destructive insects.
  • It has a powerful, long-lasting effect.
  • It provides protection to cotton and other vegetable crops from the threats posed by sucking and lepidopteran insects.
  • It spreads and acts fast, resulting in quick control of insects.

Simodis Insecticide Technical Details

  • Technical Content: Isocycloseram 9.2% w/w DC + Isocycloseram 10% w/v DC
  • Mode of Entry: Contact action
  • Mode of Action: Featuring a novel mode of action, it is classified as a Group 30 insecticide, Acts on the insect nervous system as a GABA gates chloride channel allosteric modulators.

Key Features and Benefits

  • Simodis is a broad-spectrum insecticide, precisely targets a wide range of pests, including Thrips, mites, jassids & lepidopteran pests etc.
  • Simodis Insecticide is a pest control method that is effective against all stages of the pest’s life cycle from the immature stage to the adult stage.
  • It is effective against sucking, chewing & biting feeding type of pests.
  • It has excellent translaminar action. The active ingredient in Simodis, sprayed on the upper surface of leaf, immediately percolates down to lower surface of the leaf to control pests.
  • Simodis Insecticide has excellent sunlight stability and rain-resistant properties, providing extended residual activity.
