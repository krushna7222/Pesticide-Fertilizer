Product Description

About Product

  • Phoskill Insecticide is a systemic insecticide. It belongs to the Organophosphate group of insecticides.
  • Phoskill Insecticide has a quick knockdown effect and provides long-lasting control of pests .

Phoskill Insecticide Technical Details

  • Technical Content: Monocrotophos 36% SL
  • Mode of Entry: Systemic
  • Mode of Action: ACHE Inhibitor is the key role, Monocrotophos disrupts the nervous system of pests, effectively controlling a wide range of insects that threaten crop health.

Key Features and Benefits

  • Simodis is a broad-spectrum insecticide, precisely targets a wide range of pests, including Thrips, mites, jassids & lepidopteran pests etc.Phoskill Insecticide is a chemical pesticide highly effective broad-spectrum solution for the management of various sucking and chewing pests in Agriculture.
  • Phoskill quick knock down control at very affordable cost of treatment per acre.
